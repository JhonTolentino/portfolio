Branding - Package Design

They say that “pictures speak louder than words”; this quote has become even more true in this day of technological advancements. With cameras becoming part of an integral part of visual communication, it makes sense to have higher quality equipment for important occasions.

With better quality lenses that capture every minute detail in those perfect moments, you consumers expect the packaging to match the product. With the goal of not decreasing the bottom-line, creating a biodegradable or recyclable material and changing how to better present the product. Drawbridge box is the most optimum way of holding and transporting camera lenses.

With this concept, the process of choosing and unboxing the lenses is one whole experience in itself. With reusable pieces added in tot he packaging helps entice consumers to practise company loyalty. Quality products need to packaged in a quality manner, the experience is what keeps and grows consumers.
